Simple workflow

From blank canvas to production report

Three steps to a fully documented LED wall build your crew can actually work from.

1

Build Your Wall

Choose your tile panels from the built-in library and place them on a pixel-accurate canvas. Mix brands, rotate tiles, and build multiple walls in a single project.

2

Map Data & Power

Assign processors, route data ports with auto chain numbering, and map power circuits. Load calculations happen automatically so you never blow a breaker mid-show.

3

Export Your Report

Generate pixel maps, data flow diagrams, power flow diagrams, and full PDF production reports. Hand them to your crew and get to work.

Features

Everything you need on show day

Built by people who have run cables in the dark. Every feature exists because someone needed it on a real gig.

📦

Tile & Processor Library

Hundreds of tiles from ROE Visual, Absen, Brompton, Novastar, and more. Plus every major processor — MCTRL4K, Brompton SX40, Novastar VX series, and XD expander configurations.

🏗️

Multi-Wall Projects

Build every wall at your event in one project. Multiple canvases, multiple walls — grouped together or spread out. Switch between them instantly with the wall tab bar.

🔌

Data Port Routing

Assign tiles to processor ports visually. Chain numbers are calculated automatically. See your full data flow at a glance — color-coded by port with pixel capacity tracking.

⚡

Power Circuit Mapping

Route power circuits and watch the load calculate in real time. Know exactly how much draw each circuit carries before you plug anything in. No more tripped breakers.

📄

Full Export Suite

PNG pixel maps, data flow diagrams, power flow diagrams, CSV production reports, and full PDF reports — everything your crew needs, in the format they need it.

✈️

Works Fully Offline

No internet required after activation. Use it in the venue, on the truck, or anywhere you need it — your project files live on your machine, not a server.
